🧠 Brain Master: Logic & Speed — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Brain Master: Logic & Speed ("we", "our", or "the app") is a brain training and mind game developed by Thanh Nguyen. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

📋 Information We Collect

Account Information

  • When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from your provider.
  • If you play as a Guest, we create an anonymous account. No personal information is collected.

Game Data

  • Game scores, history, streaks, and settings.
  • This data is stored locally on your device and synced to our cloud servers (Firebase) if you are signed in.

Device Information

  • Device type, operating system version, and a unique device identifier for push notifications (FCM token).

🎯 How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the service: Save your game progress, display leaderboards, and sync data across devices.
  • Push notifications: Send daily challenges, streak reminders, and engagement messages (you can opt out at any time).
  • Analytics: Understand how the app is used to improve the experience (via Firebase Analytics).
  • Advertising: Display personalized or non-personalized ads via Google AdMob.
