Frequently Asked Questions

My progress is not saving. What should I do?

Make sure you are signed in with your account (Apple, Google, or Email). Guest progress is stored locally on your device and may be lost if the app is deleted.

The app is crashing or not loading correctly.

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, restart your device. Ensure your device is running iOS 15 or later. Contact us if the problem continues.

How do I turn off notifications?

Go to Settings → Notifications → Brain Master and disable notifications. You can also manage notifications inside the app under Settings.

How do I opt out of personalized ads?

On iOS, go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and disable tracking for Brain Master. You can also select “Ask App Not to Track” when prompted by the App Tracking Transparency dialog.

My high score is not showing on the leaderboard.

Leaderboards sync when you are signed in and connected to the internet. Ensure you are logged into your account and have a stable connection.

How to Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly within the app:

  1. Open the app and go to Settings
  2. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  3. Confirm the deletion

This action permanently removes your profile, game progress, scores, and authentication data. Deleted accounts cannot be restored.

You may also request account deletion by contacting us at nguyenvanthanh.cs94@gmail.com.
