❓ Frequently Asked Questions

My progress is not saving. What should I do?

Make sure you are signed in with a Google or Apple account. Guest accounts only save data locally on your device. Try signing out and back in to trigger a sync.

The app is crashing or not loading correctly.

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, try uninstalling and reinstalling. Make sure your device is running iOS 15 or later. Contact us if the problem continues.

How do I turn off notifications?

Go to your device's Settings → Notifications → Brain Master and toggle off notifications. You can also manage this inside the app under Settings → Notifications.

How do I opt out of personalized ads?

On iOS, go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and disable tracking for Brain Master. You can also choose "Ask App Not to Track" when prompted by the app.

My high score is not showing on the leaderboard.

Leaderboard scores are synced to the cloud when you are signed in. Make sure you have a stable internet connection and are logged in with your account.

🗑️ How to Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from within the app:

  1. Open the app and go to Settings (gear icon)
  2. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  3. Confirm the deletion in the dialog

This will permanently remove your profile, game history, scores, and authentication data. This action cannot be undone.

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing us at nguyenvanthanh.cs94@gmail.com.
